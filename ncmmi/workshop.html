Workshops

Hands on Expirence with NS3

The theme of the workshop is to create an open simulation environment to provide comprehensive understanding of ns-3 and highlight its scope for networking research. ns-3 supports simulation of emerging technologies like LTE/ LTE-A/ 5G. ns-3 allows many existing real world protocol to be implemented & emulated practically.

Key Highlights

  • Differentiation of ns-2 & ns-3
  • Introduction to ns-3
  • Understanding the architecture of ns-3
  • Simulating Mobility Models in ns-3
  • Applying Patches to ns-3

26th April 2016
9:00 am - 11:00 am
Academic Block, VIT Chennai
